◆ angle()

scalar Direction::angle ( Direction const &  d2) const

Find the angle in radians between THIS unit/direction vector and another.

Calculate the angle between two vectors. The angle is the arctangent of: the vector magnitude of: the cross product of d1 and d2 divided by: the dot product of d1 and d2.

ATAN2 takes 2 arguments so that we avoid division. Since the vectors must be normalized, the parameters are given as direction vectors.
